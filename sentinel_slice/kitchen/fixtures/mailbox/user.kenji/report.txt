Quarterly Operations Report — Q3

The onboarding pipeline cleared 142 accounts this quarter, up 18% over Q2.
Mean time-to-activation fell from 9.1 to 7.4 days. Two incidents were logged,
both resolved within SLA. Headcount is flat; tooling spend rose modestly on
the new review-queue automation.

Recommendation: hold the current staffing plan and revisit automation ROI at
the end of Q4.
